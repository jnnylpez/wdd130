Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Wildpedia is to provide a captivating and educational online platform that ignites the passion for animals, fostering a sense of connection and understanding between humans and the animal kingdom. We aim to promote compassion, raise awareness about endangered species, and inspire through stories of animal resilience, creating a virtual menagerie that transports users to the heart of the animal world.

Audience

Wildpedia is specifically designed to captivate and educate children, offering them a unique and immersive online experience centered around the animal kingdom. Our engaging articles, stunning photos, live videos, and educational videos are tailored to pique their curiosity, expand their knowledge, and cultivate a love for wildlife. By connecting children with the wonders of the natural world, we aim to inspire empathy, foster a sense of responsibility for conservation, and empower the next generation to become compassionate stewards of our planet. Adventure-seeking individuals aged 18-45, financially in the middle to upper-middle-income brackets, with a passion for outdoor activities and a desire for thrilling experiences in nature. They seek comprehensive information, expert guidance, and safety measures in their river rafting adventures. They access the website through mobile devices and desktop/laptop computers for trip planning and research. -->
